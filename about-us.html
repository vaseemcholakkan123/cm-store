About CM Travel Point.com

Discover unbeatable travel experiences with us, where customer satisfaction and expert service are our top priorities.

Located in the heart of Malappuram, Kerala, inside VPC Mall behind Yummy Broast, CM Travel Point is your trusted partner for all things travel. We specialize in offering personalized services such as airline ticketing, visa assistance, holiday packages, and more. Whether you’re planning a local trip or an international adventure, we are here to make your travel seamless and stress-free.

Our Vision

With a commitment to excellence, we aim to connect people to the world, one trip at a time.

At CM Travel Point, our vision is to make travel accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone. We strive to become a trusted leader in the travel industry by offering exceptional service, personalized travel solutions, and a seamless booking experience. Our goal is to simplify the travel process, ensuring that every journey is memorable and stress-free.
